Ask a question ↗
Do I need a local entity to sell in Japan?

Not always. Many brands begin through cross-border e-commerce or marketplace channels without a Japanese subsidiary. We evaluate entry models based on your category, scale, compliance needs, and long-term ambitions — and recommend the lowest-friction path that meets your goals.

How long does a Japan market research engagement take?

Initial research sprints typically run 2–4 weeks depending on category complexity, data availability, and scope. We provide a fixed timeline and deliverable list before work begins — no open-ended retainers without defined outputs.

Can you help with Japanese product listings and copy?

Yes. Localization is a core service. We adapt messaging for Japanese buyers — culturally aligned, keyword-aware, and conversion-ready. This is not machine translation; it's strategic copy adaptation for your category and channel.

What is included in the Business Opportunity Report?

The report includes an executive summary, market sizing, competitive matrix, pricing analysis, channel recommendations, localization requirements, operational considerations, and a phased launch roadmap — culminating in a clear go / pivot / wait recommendation.

What industries does Olat Studio work with?

We specialize in e-commerce, resale, collectibles, and niche consumer categories — areas where pricing intelligence, competitive positioning, and operational data drive commercial outcomes rather than brand awareness alone.

How do we get started?

Email contact.olatstudio@gmail.com with your product, target audience, and timeline. We respond within two business days with an honest assessment of fit and suggested next steps — typically a 30-minute discovery call.
